## 2. POST STRUCTURE CATALOG: 2026 FORMAT ARCHITECTURE

### 1. Text-Only (The 1300-Char Sweet Spot)
*   **Target:** 1,100–1,300 characters.
*   **Visual Formatting:** Single-line hooks, alternating 1-2 sentence paragraphs.
*   **Rationale:** Maximizes "See More" clicks. Focuses on the "Human Fingerprint" through narrative depth.

### 2. Document Carousel (The Reach King)
*   **Target:** 6–12 slides.
*   **Visual Formatting:** Vertical (1080x1350) for maximum mobile real estate. High-contrast typography.
*   **Slide Flow:** Hook -> Agitation -> Framework -> Proof -> CTA.
*   **Rationale:** Generates 3.2x more reach. Every swipe is an interaction signal that exponentially inflates the Depth Score.

### 3. Video Post (Long-Form Retention)
*   **Target:** 90–180 seconds.
*   **Visual Formatting:** Native upload with hard-coded captions (80% of users watch sound-off).
*   **Rationale:** **Depth Score = (Dwell Time × 2...)**. High-retention long-form video outranks short clips by signaling deeper expertise to the Knowledge Graph.

### 4. Poll Post (The Conversation Starter)
*   **Target:** 1-sentence provocation + 3 specific options.
*   **Visual Formatting:** Clear, non-biased options.
*   **Rationale:** Low-friction engagement that triggers "Member Activity" signals for the initial network test.

### 5. Long-Form Thought Piece (Authority Deep Dive)
*   **Target:** 2,000–3,000 characters.
*   **Visual Formatting:** TL;DR summary block at the top; bolded H2 headers.
*   **Rationale:** Establishes "Topical Authority" and provides the AI-Scraping "Direct Answer Blocks" required for LLM citations.

### 6. Case Study (The Transformation Loop)
*   **Target:** P-A-R-L (Problem-Approach-Result-Lesson) structure.
*   **Visual Formatting:** Bulleted "Before vs. After" metrics.
*   **Rationale:** Bypasses buyer skepticism. High "Save" rate which carries a 5x algorithmic weight.

### 7. Newsletter Tease (The Gateway)
*   **Target:** 500–800 characters.
*   **Visual Formatting:** No body links. Directs to "Featured Section" or "Bio."
*   **Rationale:** Avoids the 60% reach penalty associated with outbound links while maintaining a 5.6x follower growth rate.

## 5. FORMATTING RULES 2026: ALGORITHM COMPLIANCE

| Feature | 2026 Compliance Rule | Rationale |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Emoji Strategy** | Max 2-3 per post. Use for bullets only. | High "Human-to-Corporate" ratio; over-use triggers the "Low Quality" classifier. |
| **Unicode/Fonts** | Standard text ONLY. Use **Bold** sparingly. | Non-standard Unicode (fancy fonts) breaks screen readers and lowers Depth Scores. |
| **Hashtag Strategy** | 3–5 hashtags maximum. | More than 5 hashtags are flagged as spam/engagement bait by NLP filters. |
| **Hashtag Mix** | 1 Broad : 2 Niche : 1 Branded/Personal. | Balances broad reach with specific Knowledge Graph entity mapping. |
| **External Links** | Never in the post body. Use Bio/Comments. | Body links trigger a ~60% reach penalty (External Link Penalty). |
| **Image Quality** | Human-centric, candid, or high-contrast. | Candid photos build "Entity Trust" more effectively than generic stock. |

## 6. BLOG-TO-LINKEDIN CONVERSION PROTOCOLS

1.  **The "One Big Idea" Extraction:** Do not attempt a full summary. Select the single most "citable" data point (e.g., "AI reduces collective creative diversity") to own the Depth Score.
2.  **Header-to-Hook Conversion:** Rewriting descriptive blog headers (e.g., "The Three Pillars") into curiosity-gap or contrarian hooks (e.g., "Why your competitor’s strategy looks exactly like yours").
3.  **The "Bounce Rate" Mitigation:** Ensure the first 3 lines provide enough value to prevent a bounce but enough curiosity to force the "See More" click.
4.  **The "Human Fingerprint" Overlay:** Inject a personal "war story" or "hard-won lesson" (e.g., a specific meeting where AI-generated content failed) to bypass the "AI Slop" filter.
